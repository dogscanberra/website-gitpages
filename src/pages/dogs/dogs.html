Walk a Dog
To walk our dogs, you will first need to become a Dogs Canberra Community Member. To do so, please fill out our Community Member Expression of Interest Form and we will email you our Dogs Canberra OHS Guidelines, Dog Walking Manual and Community Members Induction.

Once signed and returned to us, we will approve you as a Community Member and give you a member ID which you can use to access our dog walking service!
Foster or Adopt
We are strong believers in community and support our foster carers by assisting with set up and providing ongoing training and support.

We take time and care with our adoption process to ensure our pups find the best possible homes.

If you are interested in joining our network of foster carers or would like to adopt one of our dogs, please fill out our Foster and Adoption Expression of Interest Form.
