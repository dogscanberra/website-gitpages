Walk a Dog
To walk our dogs, you will need to become a Dogs Canberra member. This involves reading our guidelines and policies, and passing a short test.

After becoming a member, you will be given a member ID number, which can be used to access our dog walking service here.
Foster a Dog
We are strong believers in community and support our foster carers by assisting with set up costs and providing ongoing training and support. If you would to join our network of foster carers, please fill out the Google Form below.
Adopt a Dog
If you are interested in adopting any of our dogs, please get in contact via our general@dogscbr.org.au email and we will set up a time to meet!
