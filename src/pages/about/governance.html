Governance

Dogs Canberra Limited is incorporated as a Public Company Limited by Guarantee and is a Registered Charity under the Australian Charities and Not-for-profits Commission (ACNC).

This status requires us to have members and hold meetings from time to time with an elected council of governing Board Members. The Board is led by the Managing Director.

We are governed by our Constitution, and our Regulations, Policies, and By-Laws. These documents are available on our Drive
