Who are we?

Dogs Canberra (DogsCBR) is a registered charity dedicated to improving the quality of life for kennel dogs while enriching public mental health and community well-being.

Our kennel-free model offers a safe and nurturing environment where dogs can recover from the stress and trauma of traditional dog shelters.
Meet the Team

What do we do?

In a perfect world, no dog would ever know the confines of a kennel. While this dream may be beyond reach, our mission is clear: to minimise every dog's time behind bars.

We take a small number of dogs from shelters and pounds in the ACT and regional NSW, focusing on building their resilience and temperament through professional assessment and training.

Here, they find more than shelter—they find healing in a nurturing environment where they can recover from the trauma of traditional kennels.
Once ready, our dogs join our community network, where volunteers walk, play, and bond with them.

Dogs receive the love they deserve, while community members who cannot have pets of their own experience the joy and therapeutic benefits of animal companionship.

These meaningful connections create a ripple effect of healing. Our approach aims to significantly reduce return rates and helps ease the burden on overcrowded shelters.

Every dog deserves a second chance to thrive. With your support, we can continue to transform lives, one paw at a time.
