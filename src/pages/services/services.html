Walk a Dog
To walk our dogs, you will first need to become a Dogs Canberra Community Member. To do so, please fill out our Community Member Expression of Interest Form and we will email you our Dogs Canberra OHS Guidelines, Dog Walking Manual and Community Members Induction.

Once signed and returned to us, we will approve you as a Community Member and give you a member ID which you can use to access our dog walking service!
Foster a Dog
We are strong believers in community and support our foster carers by assisting with set up and providing ongoing training and support. If you are interested in joining our network of foster carers, please fill out our Foster Program Expression of Interest Form.
Adopt a Dog
If you are interested in adopting any of our dogs, please get in contact via our general email and we will set up a time to meet!
