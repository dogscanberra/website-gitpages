What is our Community Walking Program?

Our Community Walking Program is a service which allows people from all walks of life to spend time with a canine companion. You can go for a walk, play a game of fetch, or just spend some time socialising with one of our furry friends. Walking a dog is good for you, the dog, and the community!

Why Walk a Dog?

It's Good for You!
Walking a dog can help you to feel refreshed and revitalised, and promotes both physical and mental health. The non-judgmental presence of a dog can provide a deep sense of companionship and emotional support.

It's Good for the Dog!
Our dogs will benefit greatly from socialisation and exercise. By spending time with a range of people our dogs will learn more about the world around them, helping to prepare them for adoption.

It's Good for the Community!
When you walk the dogs in our Community Walking Program, you are supporting our foster carers with the great work they do. Getting out and about also brings awareness to our mission and promotes wellbeing throughout the community.

How Do I Sign Up?

To join our Community Walking Program you will need to become a Community Member. To do so, click the button below to fill out an Expression of Interest form. We will send you an Induction and short quiz on our OHS procedures. Once signed and completed we will send you a Community Membership Number. Once you receive a Community Membership Number you can select an eligible dog and book in a time to walk them!
